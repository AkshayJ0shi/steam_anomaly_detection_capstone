Hello,

We want to give you a chance in hosting and administrating your own Linux Immune server, to open your region up for other Immune players. (Hello to all the new Brazil players from the last days!)

About This Game

Experience true survival with a wink in the world of Immune - the new open-world online game.
Survive the wastelands with the help of your friends and your hand crafted weapons. Build your own fort to defend yourself and your hard earned loot from evil mutants, bandits or reckless players.

Features:

  • Multiplayer survival with XP, ranks and missions.
  • Optional PVP and PVE with wild animals, soldiers and mutants.
  • Craft weapons, clothing or tools and build your own fort.
  • Plant, grow and harvest your own food or just go fishing.
  • Open cases and collect, trade or win rare Steam Inventory items to customize your character.
  • Health conditions, medicine, vehicles, trading and much more to discover.

XP: Experience Points
PVP: Player Versus Player
PVE: Player Versus Environment

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows XP or later
    • Processor: 2 GHz, Dual Core
    • Memory: 2 GB RAM
    • Graphics: DirectX 9 supported, less than 7 years old
    • DirectX: Version 9.0c
    • Network: Broadband Internet connection
    • Storage: 400 MB available space
    Recommended:
    • OS: Windows XP or later
    • Processor: 2 GHz, Dual Core
    • Memory: 4 GB RAM
    • Graphics: DirectX 10 supported, less than 5 years old
    • DirectX: Version 10
    • Network: Broadband Internet connection
    • Storage: 400 MB available space
    • Sound Card: yes
