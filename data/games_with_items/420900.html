Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“This is our first online multiplayer project. Do not be alarmed, Mech Anarchy has proven to be stable with many players from all over the world. We wish to continue to develop and evolve Mech Anrchy alongside our community. Our goal is to be open and transparent while we put the finishing touches on our game.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We anticipate being in Early Access for at least six months. We will gather data and use any funds earned to improve the game.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“We would like to add numerous features including:
  • A full story mode.
  • Boss battles.
  • A Steam Workshop integrated level editor.
  • Clans with organized clan battles.
  • In Game voice.
  • More Unlockables.
  • More Game modes.
”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The current state of Mech Anarchy is very stable and loads of fun to play with friends/frenemies. It ships as an arena fighter with:
  • Over 100 unlockables.
  • 10 Unique game modes.
  • Up to 8 Players Online.
  • 5 Player local with seamless transition to Online.
”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We plan to gradually raise the price as we ship new content and features.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We are extremely interested in your feedback. Every question or critic will be taken seriously and answered promptly.”

About This Game

MECH ANARCHY is an action packed, fast-paced top down shooter created by intense gamers, for intense gamers. Arcade style shooting with near limitless customization makes no two battles alike. Supporting up to 10 player local and online play, MECH ANARCHY supports all kinds of gamers with a pick up and get in to the action mentality.

Feel the adrenaline run through you with multiple different weapons, game modes, and bonus rounds! Team up with your friends or engage in all out chaos with this new action packed game. Paint your mechs, gear up and head into the frenzy that is MECH ANARCHY.

Features:

  • Full controller Support
  • Hundreds of unlockables
  • 1-5 players (co-op or competitive)
  • 2-8 Player Online Multiplayer
  • Seamless transition from local to online gameplay
  • Hundreds of Unique Levels
  • Bullets that curve towards enemies
  • Various special types of ammunition
  • Dashing
  • Melee Combat
  • Limited Ammo
  • Bullet Catching
  • 1 Hit Kills
  • Extreme skill-based gameplay

Bullets Seek

lightly toward opponents. But use them well - you only have a few. Pull your enemies' missed shots from the walls and send them flying back, or retrieve yours from the debris of your foes.

In Chests

you may find powerful varieties of bullets. Savvy opponents know to strike when you over-extend yourself for treasure greedily.

Dashing

is key to escaping tight situations, but you are left vulnerable after. Particularly skilled Mech pilots will pluck bullets from mid-air as they sail past.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • Memory: 2 GB RAM
    • DirectX: Version 9.0
    • Network: Broadband Internet connection
    • Storage: 60 MB available space
    Recommended:
    • Memory: 4 GB RAM
    • DirectX: Version 9.0
    • Storage: 1 GB available space
