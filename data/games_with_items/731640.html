About This Game

A simple arcade game for two players. Pick up knives, throw them, dodge them. Compete against other players, get new things, customize your character. Nothing else is needed for a great play.

Features:
  • Fast gameplay
  • Steam inventory system
  • Character сustomization
  • Pleasant graphics and soundtrack
