Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We have much to do, the game lacks many characters and maps.
In the game, not all things that we would like were reanalyzed.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We plan to release the game in the third quarter of 2018”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“We are going to add:
- new characters (lesbians, transsexuals and bisexuals)
- new maps
- vr mode”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The game has several different characters, one battlegrounds map and work bots.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“After exiting early access, the price will increase about five times, it is also possible that the price will rise during development”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We will collect complaints from the community about errors in the game, as well as ideas for new characters and maps.
Communication with the players will be carried out through discussions steam.”

About This Game

The royal battle is now also for the LGBT community.

Your task is to choose one character and start playing.

There are several options:
- Play alone with bots


The essence of the "Royal Battle" regime - players must destroy each other, the last survivor is the winner.
But it is not so easy to win, at first everyone descends to an uninhabited island and is stocked with objects (weapons), then they start killing each other.

BUT ALL THE SAVED PLAYERS ARE GAYS.
As a bunch of first-class gays can withstand and kill their fellow-orientation.
