About This Game

A witch imprisoned by a divine appointment weeps eternally all her spoils. Helena was transported to the place. This nightmare that can last forever and you have to help this girl avoid unwanted items, consume heart and potions to control the fear, collect treasures and take risks to stay alive.

Unique Game Mode

Survivor - Every second her fear increases and she needs to catch the power-ups to keep the fear from growing as she collects as much as possible. Focus on picking the suggested item in the line to raise your level. Each level multiples the values of the power-ups.

Power-ups

Each power-up has a specific function like reducing fear, increasing your score or both:

  • Heart - Reduces a small amount of fear.
  • Potion - Reduces a large amount of the fear.
  • Skull - Gives 1000 points to the score but increases the fear drastically.
  • Gold, Ring and Money - Gives 500, 200 and 100 points to the score, respectively.

Good Luck!
